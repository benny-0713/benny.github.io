An invitation into my world



Exhales, here and now...

I am a Nigerian-born model and movement
director currently based in the Nigeria. As a model, movement
director, and nomad, my art sheds light on Black stories and is an
extension of who I am and where I come from, so I use it to
document, celebrate, enlighten and uplift Black people. It is a
medium through which I experience the most freedom and an
avenue to see the world. My art is also an expression of my
gratitude and servitude to the world - I love lending myself to new
thoughts and ideas and celebrating the freedom within me and beyond.

Currently roaming the world and learning new nuances.
